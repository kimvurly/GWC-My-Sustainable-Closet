Four Ways to Input Clothing into Closet

Take a photo to insert clothing, the AI will analyze the photo and confirm that the piece of clothing they identified is the correct one.

Search up the name of the piece of clothing. A very broad way of searching for clothes.

Insert a link to the clothing listing if available online, details about the piece of clothing will automatically be indentified.

Take a photo of the clothing and the user can put in the details themselves (brand, name, type of clothing, style).
